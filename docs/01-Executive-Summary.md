## Executive Summary

The HG Data Foundation project aims to establish a secure enterprise knowledge platform that centralizes company information, supports AI-assisted knowledge retrieval, and improves collaboration across departments.

Phase 1 focuses on building the data foundation, implementing secure access controls, and preparing enterprise knowledge for future AI integration.
